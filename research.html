Research Findings

Abstract

  • This study analyzes the impact of online reviews and ratings on consumer trust and purchasing behavior.
  • A structured 10-question survey as conducted among students, professionals, and regular e-commerce users.
  • The research evaluates how ratings, verified purchase badges, negative feedback, and review authenticity influence buying decisions.
  • Findings reveal that digital trust depends on transparency, credibility, and rating patterns.
  • The study highlights the importance of awareness in identifying fake reviews for safe and responsible online purchasing.

Charts & Graphs

Key Insights & Important Conclusions

  • 92% respondents prefer products with 4★ and above ratings.
  • Verified Purchase badges increase trust by 35%.
  • 78% users check negative reviews before purchasing.
  • Repetitive or overly generic comments indicate possible fake reviews.
  • Detailed reviews with images/videos are perceived as more trustworthy.
  • Consumers are willing to pay more for highly rated products.
  • Awareness programs significantly improve digital decision-making.
