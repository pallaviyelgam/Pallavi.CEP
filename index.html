Introduction / Purpose

The Review Right initiative is a community awareness program designed to educate the public about the importance of online reviews and ratings. This platform defines and elaborates on how digital reviews influence consumer behavior, build trust, and impact purchasing decisions.

Our purpose is to create community awareness about identifying genuine feedback, understanding rating systems, and protecting consumers from fake or misleading reviews.

Objectives

  • ✔ Define and explain the importance of online reviews in e-commerce.
  • ✔ Describe the targets and goals of public awareness regarding digital trust.
  • ✔ Educate users about identifying fake and manipulated reviews.
  • ✔ Promote safe, responsible, and informed purchasing decisions.
  • ✔ Encourage ethical reviewing practices in online communities.

Scope of Awareness

This awareness program covers a broad target audience, including:

  • 🎓 Students – Learning digital literacy and smart online shopping habits.
  • 👪 Parents – Protecting family members from misleading online purchases.
  • 🛒 General Public – Everyday consumers using e-commerce platforms.
  • 💻 Digital Buyers – Frequent online shoppers relying on ratings.
  • 🏢 Small Businesses – Encouraging fair and honest review practices.

The initiative focuses on building responsible digital citizens who can confidently analyze reviews and make informed decisions.
